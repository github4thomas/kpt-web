ABOUT US

KPT Precision is a full service CNC precision machining facility, manufacturing high tech, close tolerance, precision machined components from a wide variety of materials. Our services include production CNC milling and turning, prototyping and assembly.

Our highly skilled team has extensive experience in the Aerospace, Process Instruments, Medical, Robotics, Satellite Telecommunications and Plastic Injection Molding Industries.

Our 10,000 square foot climate controlled facility is located in Rexdale, Ontario Canada.

We welcome the opportunity to quote on all of your machining requirements, and thank you for your interest in KPT Precision.
